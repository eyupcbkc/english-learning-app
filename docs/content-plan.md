# Content Plan - A1 to C1 Full Curriculum

## Genel Prensipler
- Amerikan aksani (en-US) - tum ses, telaffuz ve ornekler
- Her unite gercek hayat senaryosu icermeli
- Kelimeler gunluk hayattan, is dunyasindan, seyahatten
- Ornek cumleler dogal, konusma dili - ders kitabi gibi degil
- Turkce aciklama her yerde olmali (A1-A2)
- B1'den itibaren Turkce azalir, Ingilizce artar

## STAGE 1: A1 → A2 (Unit 01-16)

### Module 1: First Steps (Unit 01-08) — "Hayatta kalma Ingilizcesi"
Hedef: Temel iletisim, kendini tanıtma, basit sorular

#### Unit 01: Greetings & Introductions ✅
- Senaryo: Kafede yeni biriyle tanisma
- Kelimeler: hello, goodbye, please, thank you, name, friend, morning, evening, fine
- Gramer: Subject pronouns + Be (I am, You are)
- Dialog: Emma ile kafede tanisma

#### Unit 02: Numbers, Days & Months ✅
- Senaryo: Okul kayit ofisinde bilgi verme
- Kelimeler: one-hundred, Monday-Sunday, January-December, today, tomorrow, yesterday
- Gramer: Numbers 1-100, How many, How old
- Dialog: Sekreterle kayit islemi

#### Unit 03: Present Simple - Be ✅
- Senaryo: Sinifta insanlari anlatma
- Kelimeler: happy, sad, tired, hungry, cold, hot, tall, short, teacher, student, doctor
- Gramer: Be fiili (+/-/?) am/is/are, isn't, aren't
- Dialog: Zeynep ile siniftakileri anlatma

#### Unit 04: Present Simple - Do
- Senaryo: **Markette alisveris - "Ne seversin?"**
- Kelimeler: like, want, need, buy, eat, drink, cook, bread, milk, fruit, vegetable, meat, rice, water, juice
- Gramer: Present Simple (+/-/?) do/does, don't/doesn't
- Dialog: Kasiyerle konusma, "Do you have...?", "I want..."
- Okuma: Haftalik alisveris listesi blog yazisi

#### Unit 05: Basic Questions
- Senaryo: **Otelde check-in**
- Kelimeler: room, key, floor, elevator, breakfast, wifi, passport, reservation, night, price
- Gramer: What, Where, When, Who, How + soru yapisi
- Dialog: Resepsiyonist ile check-in
- Okuma: Otel degerlendirmesi (review)

#### Unit 06: Daily Routines
- Senaryo: **YouTube vlogger "Bir gunüm" tarzi**
- Kelimeler: wake up, get up, brush, shower, breakfast, leave, arrive, work, lunch, dinner, sleep, exercise
- Gramer: Present Simple ile rutin anlatma, zaman ifadeleri (at 7, in the morning)
- Dialog: Arkadasina gununu anlatma
- Okuma: "A Day in My Life" blog yazisi

#### Unit 07: Food & Drinks
- Senaryo: **Restoranda siparis verme**
- Kelimeler: menu, order, waiter, bill, tip, appetizer, main course, dessert, steak, salad, coffee, tea, check
- Gramer: Would like (I'd like...), Can I have...?, some/any
- Dialog: Garsonla siparis
- Okuma: Restoran menusu + yorum

#### Unit 08: A1 Review & Test
- Senaryo: **Karma - Tum senaryolardan sorular**
- Kelimeler: Unit 01-07 tekrar (en zor 30 kelime)
- Gramer: Tum A1 grameri karisik test
- Dialog: Uzun bir senaryo - otele gidis, restoran, tanisma
- Test: 50 soruluk seviye belirleme

---

### Module 2: Expanding Basics (Unit 09-16) — "Gunluk hayat"
Hedef: Su anki ve gecmis zamanlar, karsilastirma

#### Unit 09: Present Continuous
- Senaryo: **Havalimaninda - "Su an ne yapiyorsun?"**
- Kelimeler: waiting, boarding, flying, landing, luggage, gate, passport control, delay, announcement, seat
- Gramer: am/is/are + -ing, Present Simple vs Continuous
- Dialog: Telefonda "Where are you? I am waiting at gate 12."

#### Unit 10: Can / Can't
- Senaryo: **Araba kiralama - "Yapabilirim/Yapamam"**
- Kelimeler: drive, license, insurance, automatic, manual, GPS, fill up, gas station, return, damage
- Gramer: can/can't + abilities, requests, permission
- Dialog: Rent a car ofisinde

#### Unit 11: There is / There are + Prepositions
- Senaryo: **Airbnb evi tarif etme**
- Kelimeler: kitchen, bathroom, bedroom, living room, balcony, parking, garden, furniture, towel, blanket
- Gramer: There is/are, prepositions (in, on, under, next to, between, behind)
- Dialog: Ev sahibiyle telefonda evi sorma

#### Unit 12: Past Simple - Regular
- Senaryo: **Tatil anisi - "Gecen yaz ne yaptim?"**
- Kelimeler: visited, traveled, enjoyed, stayed, walked, talked, played, watched, cooked, cleaned, called, arrived
- Gramer: -ed ekiyle Past Simple, did/didn't
- Dialog: Arkadasina tatilini anlatma

#### Unit 13: Past Simple - Irregular (Top 40)
- Senaryo: **Seyahat blog yazisi**
- Kelimeler: went, saw, ate, drank, bought, took, made, came, had, got, knew, thought, found, gave, told, left, felt, became, ran, wrote
- Gramer: Irregular verbs, Was/Were
- Dialog: "What did you do in Istanbul?"
- Okuma: Bir turistin Istanbul blog yazisi

#### Unit 14: Adjectives & Comparatives
- Senaryo: **Online alisveris - urun karsilastirma**
- Kelimeler: cheap, expensive, fast, slow, big, small, better, worse, the best, the worst, review, rating, delivery, quality
- Gramer: -er/-est, more/most, irregular (good-better-best)
- Dialog: "Which phone is better? This one is cheaper but the other one is faster."

#### Unit 15: Shopping, Money, Directions
- Senaryo: **Sehirde kaybolma + alisveris**
- Kelimeler: turn left, turn right, go straight, cross, corner, block, cash, credit card, receipt, change, discount, size, try on
- Gramer: Imperatives, How much...?, prepositions of direction
- Dialog: Yolda birine yol sorma + magazada alisveris

#### Unit 16: A2 Level Test
- Senaryo: **Buyuk senaryo - 1 gunluk seyahat**
- Havalimaninda → otele → sehirde gezme → restoran → alisveris
- 60 soruluk kapsamli test
- Tum A1-A2 grameri ve kelimeleri

## STAGE 2: A2 → B1 (Unit 17-32)

### Module 3: Grammar Expansion (Unit 17-24) — "Is dunyasina giris"

#### Unit 17: Future (will & going to)
- Senaryo: **Is gorusmesine hazirlik**
- "I will work hard." / "I am going to apply for this job."

#### Unit 18: Present Perfect
- Senaryo: **CV ve is deneyimi anlatma**
- "I have worked at three companies." / "I have lived in Istanbul for 5 years."

#### Unit 19: Present Perfect vs Past Simple
- Senaryo: **LinkedIn profil yazma**
- "I worked at X from 2020 to 2023." vs "I have been a developer since 2023."

#### Unit 20: First Conditional
- Senaryo: **E-ticaret siparis problemi**
- "If you send the wrong product, I will ask for a refund."

#### Unit 21: Modal Verbs
- Senaryo: **Lojistik - kargo takibi ve musteri hizmeti**
- "The package should arrive tomorrow." / "You must provide a tracking number."

#### Unit 22: Adverbs & Frequency
- Senaryo: **Freelancer gunlugu**
- "I always start work at 9." / "I sometimes work on weekends."

#### Unit 23: Phrasal Verbs Set 1
- Senaryo: **Startup kurma hikayesi**
- "I set up a company." / "We gave up after 6 months." / "Business picked up."

#### Unit 24: B1 Entry Test
- Is dunyasi odakli kapsamli test

### Module 4: Real Communication (Unit 25-32) — "Profesyonel iletisim"

#### Unit 25: Second Conditional
- Senaryo: **Is teklifi degerlendirme**
- "If I were you, I would accept the offer."

#### Unit 26: Passive Voice
- Senaryo: **Urun iade sureci**
- "The product was damaged." / "The refund will be processed."

#### Unit 27: Reported Speech
- Senaryo: **Toplanti ozeti**
- "She said that the deadline was Friday."

#### Unit 28: Relative Clauses
- Senaryo: **E-posta yazma - musteriye cevap**
- "The product which you ordered..." / "The person who called..."

#### Unit 29: Connectors
- Senaryo: **Sunum yapma**
- "However, the results show..." / "Although sales decreased..."

#### Unit 30: Email & Formal Writing
- Senaryo: **Sirket yazismasi**
- Resmi e-posta formatlari, Dear/Sincerely

#### Unit 31: Phrasal Verbs Set 2
- Senaryo: **Musteri sikayeti cozme**
- "We'll look into it." / "Please fill out this form."

#### Unit 32: B1 Level Test
- Profesyonel iletisim odakli kapsamli test
